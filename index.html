About Me

Welcome to my personal website. My name is Hanquan Wang, and you can call me John. I am currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Washington in the Autonomous Insect Robotics Lab under Dr. Sawyer Fuller. I earned my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering at UW with a concentration in Mechatronics.

My interests are centered around control systems, robotic manipulation, robot locomotion, and bio-inspired adaptability. I enjoy combining mechanical design, software integration, and precision control to build robotic systems that are both practical and impactful. Hope you all are having fun going through my background. I would love to chat with you in any means about anything!
